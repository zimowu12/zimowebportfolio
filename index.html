Zimo Wu


Hi, I’m Zimo. I recently graduated from the Centre for Digital Media, a graduate-level institution established by Simon Fraser University, University of British Columbia, BCIT, and Emily Carr University of Art + Design. I hold a Bachelor's of Computing degree from Queen's University, specializing in Computer Science, and major in Game Development.

As a software and game developer, I have over 4 years of experience in Unity and 5+ years experience in Python, C++ and other languages.
As a 2D artist and Game Designer, I worked on several game projects. As a content creator, I have been making videos for 5 years.

Always keeping user-centric design in mind, I aim to use my skills to create work that resonates deeply with people.
